Steps

  1. Cook the Meat In a skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add SauceStir the Tomato sauce, Onions, Garlic & Seasoning into the cooked meat. Let it simmer for a few minutes.
  3. Make the Cheese Sauce Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a saucepan, add 2 tablespoons flour, stir for 1-2 minutes. Gradually whisk in 1 cup milk until thickened, then add 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese and stir until melted.
  4. Assemble the Lasagna In a baking dish, spread a layer of the meat sauce on the bottom. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce. Spread a layer of the cheese mixture over the noodles. Repeat the layers (sauce, noodles, cheese) until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of sauce on top.
  5. Top with cheese Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese on top.
  6. BakePreheat oven to 190°C. Bake for 45 mins or until pasta is cooked and cheese is sufficently melted and bubbly
  7. Please ensure to let cool for minimum 15 minutes before cutting and serving!
