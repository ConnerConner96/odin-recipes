Scrambled Eggs

Description

A Super simple yet delicious recipe for Scrambled Eggs!

List of Ingredients

  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 2 Tablespoons Milk
  • 1 Tablespoon Butter
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Whisk Eggs In a bowl, crack 4 eggs, add 2 tablespoons of milk (if using), and season with salt and pepper. Whisk until well combined.
  2. Heat the pan In a bowl, crack 4 eggs, add 2 tablespoons of milk (if using), and season with salt and pepper. Whisk until well combined.
  3. Cook the eggs Pour the egg mixture into the skillet. Let it sit for a few seconds until it starts to set, then gently stir with a spatula, pushing the eggs from the edges to the center.
  4. Finish cooking Continue cooking, stirring occasionally, until the eggs are softly set and slightly runny (they will continue to cook off the heat).
  5. Serve Remove from heat and serve immediately.
