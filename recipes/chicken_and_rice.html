Chicken & Rice

A classic meal containing only two ingredients! Chicken & Rice!

List of Ingredients

  • Chicken (Breast of Thighs)
  • Rice
  • Chicken Broth or Water
  • Olive Oil
  • Seasoning of your choice

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil or butter over medium heat. Season 1 pound of chicken breasts with salt and pepper (and any optional seasonings). Cook the chicken for about 5-7 minutes on each side until browned and cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same skillet, add 1 cup of rice and stir for a minute. Then add 2 cups of chicken broth or water. Bring to a boil.
  3. Return the chicken to the skillet, reduce the heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes (or until the rice is cooked and has absorbed the liquid).
  4. Fluff the rice with a fork, slice the chicken, and serve together.
